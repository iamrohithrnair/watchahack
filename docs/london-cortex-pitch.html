Anomaly detection

It does not just collect data. It notices when the city behaves strangely.

Anomalies are elevated because they are truly unusual, not because they look dramatic. In simple terms, London Cortex learns what normal looks like, then flags the moments when something moves far away from that normal pattern.

Low Notice Medium High Critical

  • If a source lacks exact coordinates, the platform falls back to the relevant grid cell center.
  • When the map changes color, it is showing genuine behavioral shift, not decoration.
